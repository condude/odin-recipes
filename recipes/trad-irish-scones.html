Traditional Irish Scones

Description

This delicious bakery item comes straight from a traditional irish recipe book. Slightly sweet but buttery and fluffy, they make for a great breakfast when served with fruit jam and sweet cream or butter. I eat them for lunch too. They go fast in this house.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 3/4 cups organic unbleached flour
  • 1/2 cup organic milk
  • large pinch salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 4 1/2 tbsp. sugar
  • 8 tbsp Kerrygold butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Sieve the flour, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Using hands, rub in the butter and mix in the sugar.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat egg and milk together.
  5. Add egg and milk mixture to dry ingredients to form a loose dough.
  6. Knead lightly and flatten out to about half an inch thick.
  7. Cut into scones using round cutter.
  8. Bake at the top of the oven for about 15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with butter or cream, and jam.
